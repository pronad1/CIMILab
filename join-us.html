Open Roles

Apply for a role that matches your strengths.

Research Assistant

Support active projects across machine learning, medical informatics, and reproducible AI. Ideal for graduate or advanced undergraduate researchers with strong coding and analytical skills.

  • Collaborate on experiment design and data analysis
  • Implement models and automate evaluation pipelines
  • Prepare documentation and reproducible research artifacts

Preferred background: Python, ML frameworks, statistics, healthcare data.

Apply for Research Assistant

Lab Member

Join the core research team and contribute to ongoing publications, code releases, and lab initiatives. Suited for self-motivated students or professionals who want hands-on research experience.

  • Participate in weekly lab meetings and research reviews
  • Drive experiments, reporting, and collaborative writing
  • Help maintain reproducible workflows and data assets

Preferred background: research collaboration, software skills, scientific communication.

Technical Handler

Support the lab’s technical infrastructure by managing code repositories, datasets, compute resources, and experiment tracking systems.

  • Maintain reproducible code and experiment pipelines
  • Manage data access, versioning, and compute environments
  • Support deployment and system-level research tooling

Preferred background: DevOps, Python, containerization, cloud or cluster compute.

Apply for Technical Handler

Visiting Researcher

Collaborate with CIMILab on specific research projects or short-term initiatives. Visiting appointments are ideal for researchers with complementary expertise.

  • Engage in focused research collaborations
  • Contribute to joint publications and technical reports
  • Share domain knowledge across our research agenda

Preferred background: demonstrated research record, collaborative partnerships, domain expertise.

How to apply

A high-quality application has clarity, relevance, and care.

Send your application to deycimilab@gmail.com with a clear subject line and the documents below. We prioritize candidates who explain how their skills map to our lab's research agenda.

  1. Subject line: Role title + your name (e.g., "Research Assistant Application - Jane Doe").
  2. CV / Resume: Emphasize research projects, technical skills, and relevant coursework.
  3. Cover note: Describe your motivations, preferred role, and how you can contribute to CIMILab.
  4. Supporting documents: transcripts, portfolio links, relevant publications, or code samples.

If you have multiple role interests, mention your top two preferences and why each is a good fit. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

Application essentials

  • Email: deycimilab@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 3 weeks for strong applications
  • Application type: Remote and on-campus collaboration available
  • Follow-up: One polite inquiry only if you have not heard back after 3 weeks
