Our Team

The people behind the papers

CIMILab is home to researchers from the MU Institute for Data Science and Informatics at the University of Missouri, united by a passion for trustworthy, reproducible AI.

What the lab looks like

A small but ambitious research group focused on trustworthy, impactful AI

The team is still growing, but the emphasis is clear: strong technical depth, careful thinking, and a collaborative research culture.

Research-driven

Members are encouraged to build strong technical intuition and contribute to meaningful, publication-quality work.

Collaborative culture

We value shared learning, open exchange of ideas, and a practical approach to problem solving.

Open to strong applicants

We welcome students and researchers who care about rigor, clarity, and building useful systems.
